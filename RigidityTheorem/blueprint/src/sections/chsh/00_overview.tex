\chapter{Robust Rigidity of CHSH}
\label{ch:robust-chsh}

This blueprint records the approximately extremal CHSH rigidity argument formalized in
`RigidityTheorem/Approximate_Rigidity`.
Its mathematical model follows Chapter 11 of the QIC 890 notes, while the dependency structure is taken from the Lean files that currently remain in the repository.

The formal story has three layers:

\begin{itemize}
\item a reusable quantum library for qubits, Pauli operators, Bell states, and norm estimates;
\item local isometry constructions and approximate extracted-operator relations for a near-optimal CHSH strategy;
\item a spectral argument showing that the extracted state is close to a Bell pair tensored with a junk register, followed by operator extraction for Alice and Bob.
\end{itemize}

The key Lean files are:

\begin{itemize}
\item `BasicDefs.lean` for CHSH strategies and tensor-analytic scaffolding;
\item `Isometries.lean` and `IsometriesApprox.lean` for the exact and approximate extracted-operator identities;
\item `chsh_link.lean` for the comparison between the physical CHSH bias and the ideal Bell-space operator $K$;
\item `StateExtraction.lean` and `RigidityTheorem.lean` for the spectral extraction bound and the final rigidity statements.
\end{itemize}
