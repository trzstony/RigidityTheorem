\chapter{From CHSH to guessing probability}
\label{ch:chsh-pguess}

This chapter gives a self-contained blueprint proof of the implication
\[
\varepsilon \le 2\sqrt{2}-2
\quad\Longrightarrow\quad
\Tr(\rho_{AB}\,\mathrm{CHSH})\ge 2\sqrt{2}-\varepsilon
\quad\Longrightarrow\quad
p_{\mathrm{guess}}(X\mid E)_{\rho}\le \tfrac12+O(\sqrt\varepsilon),
\]
for qubit systems $A$ and $B$, where $X$ is Alice's outcome when measuring in the key basis.
The key idea is to
\emph{postselect} on the (rank-one) Bell projector on $AB$ and use a gentle-measurement bound.

\medskip

All statements are written in a way that is compatible with the dependency-graph style used by the blueprint.
