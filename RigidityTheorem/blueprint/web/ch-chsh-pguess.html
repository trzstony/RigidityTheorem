1 From CHSH to guessing probability

This chapter gives a self-contained blueprint proof of the implication

\[ \varepsilon \le 2\sqrt{2}-2 \quad \Longrightarrow \quad \operatorname {Tr}(\rho _{AB}\, \mathrm{CHSH})\ge 2\sqrt{2}-\varepsilon \quad \Longrightarrow \quad p_{\mathrm{guess}}(X\mid E)_{\rho }\le \tfrac 12+O(\sqrt\varepsilon ), \]

for qubit systems \(A\) and \(B\), where \(X\) is Alice’s outcome when measuring in the key basis. The key idea is to postselect on the (rank-one) Bell projector on \(AB\) and use a gentle-measurement bound.

All statements are written in a way that is compatible with the dependency-graph style used by the blueprint.
