With the canonical choice of observables in Definition 3,

\[ \mathrm{CHSH} = \sqrt2\, (Z\otimes Z + X\otimes X). \]
Proof ▼

Using \(B_0+B_1=\tfrac {2Z}{\sqrt2}\) and \(B_0-B_1=\tfrac {2X}{\sqrt2}\),

\[ \mathrm{CHSH}=Z\otimes (B_0+B_1)+X\otimes (B_0-B_1)=\sqrt2\, (Z\otimes Z + X\otimes X). \]

Let \(\Phi :=|\Phi ^+\rangle \! \langle \Phi ^+|\). Then \(|\Phi ^+\rangle \) is an eigenvector of \(\mathrm{CHSH}\) with eigenvalue \(2\sqrt2\). Moreover, the spectrum of \(\mathrm{CHSH}\) is

\[ \{ 2\sqrt2,\ 0,\ 0,\ -2\sqrt2\} , \]

and the top eigenvalue \(2\sqrt2\) is non-degenerate.

Proof ▼

In the Bell basis \(\{ |\Phi ^\pm \rangle ,|\Psi ^\pm \rangle \} \), one checks that \(Z\otimes Z\) has eigenvalue \(+1\) on \(|\Phi ^\pm \rangle \) and \(-1\) on \(|\Psi ^\pm \rangle \), while \(X\otimes X\) has eigenvalue \(+1\) on \(|\Phi ^+\rangle ,|\Psi ^+\rangle \) and \(-1\) on \(|\Phi ^-\rangle ,|\Psi ^-\rangle \). Using Lemma 6 gives the stated spectrum.
