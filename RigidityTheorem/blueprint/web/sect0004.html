Theorem 15 Approximate anti-commutation
#

Uses

  • Theorem 14
  • Definition 3

Lean declarations

  • Approximate_Rigidity.approx_anticomm_A
  • Approximate_Rigidity.approx_anticomm_B

If \(\operatorname {Bias}(S) \ge 2\sqrt{2} - \varepsilon \), then both local anti-commutators are small on the strategy state. More precisely, the Lean development proves

\[ \Re \langle \psi , ((A_1A_0 + A_0A_1)^2 \otimes \mathrm{Id})\psi \rangle \le c_{\mathrm{CHSH}}\varepsilon , \]

and the analogous bound with Alice and Bob swapped.
