With the notation of Definition 9, there exists a state \(\tau _E\) on \(E\) such that

\[ \widetilde\rho _{ABE} = \Phi \otimes \tau _E. \]

Moreover, one can take

\[ \tau _E = \frac{1}{p}\, (\langle \Phi ^+|_{AB}\otimes I_E)\rho _{ABE}(|\Phi ^+\rangle _{AB}\otimes I_E). \]
Proof ▼

Since \(\Phi \) is a rank-one projector, the operator \(\Pi \rho _{ABE}\Pi \) has support contained in the one-dimensional subspace \(\mathrm{span}\{ |\Phi ^+\rangle \} \) on \(AB\). Hence there is a positive semidefinite operator \(\tau '_E\) on \(E\) such that \( \Pi \rho _{ABE}\Pi =\Phi \otimes \tau '_E \). Taking the partial matrix element of \(\rho _{ABE}\) in the vector \(|\Phi ^+\rangle \) yields the explicit formula for \(\tau '_E\) and gives \(\operatorname {Tr}(\tau '_E)=p\). Normalising \(\tau _E:=\tau '_E/p\) gives the claim.
