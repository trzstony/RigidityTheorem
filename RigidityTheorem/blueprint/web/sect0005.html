1.5 A gentle-measurement bound for Bell postselection

Lemma 11 Gentle postselection bound
#

Let \(\Pi \) and \(p=\operatorname {Tr}(\Pi \rho _{ABE})\) be as in Definition 9, and set \(\widetilde\rho _{ABE}=\Pi \rho _{ABE}\Pi /p\). Then

\[ \| \rho _{ABE}-\widetilde\rho _{ABE}\| _1 \le 2\sqrt{1-p}+2(1-p). \]
Proof ▼

Write \(\Pi ^\perp :=I-\Pi \). Decompose

\[ \rho _{ABE}-\Pi \rho _{ABE}\Pi = \Pi \rho _{ABE}\Pi ^\perp + \Pi ^\perp \rho _{ABE}\Pi + \Pi ^\perp \rho _{ABE}\Pi ^\perp . \]

By triangle inequality,

\[ \| \rho _{ABE}-\Pi \rho _{ABE}\Pi \| _1 \le \| \Pi \rho _{ABE}\Pi ^\perp \| _1+\| \Pi ^\perp \rho _{ABE}\Pi \| _1 + \operatorname {Tr}(\Pi ^\perp \rho _{ABE}). \]

The last term equals \(1-p\). For the cross term, use the Schatten inequality \(\| AB\| _1\le \| A\| _2\| B\| _2\) with \(A:=\Pi \rho _{ABE}^{1/2}\) and \(B:=\rho _{ABE}^{1/2}\Pi ^\perp \). Then \( \| A\| _2^2=\operatorname {Tr}(\Pi \rho _{ABE})=p \) and \( \| B\| _2^2=\operatorname {Tr}(\Pi ^\perp \rho _{ABE})=1-p \), so \( \| \Pi \rho _{ABE}\Pi ^\perp \| _1\le \sqrt{p(1-p)}\le \sqrt{1-p}. \) The same bound holds for \(\| \Pi ^\perp \rho _{ABE}\Pi \| _1\). Hence \(\| \rho _{ABE}-\Pi \rho _{ABE}\Pi \| _1\le 2\sqrt{1-p}+(1-p)\). Finally, since \(\widetilde\rho _{ABE}=\Pi \rho _{ABE}\Pi /p\) and \(\| \Pi \rho _{ABE}\Pi - \widetilde\rho _{ABE}\| _1 = |1-p|\), another triangle inequality gives the stated bound.
