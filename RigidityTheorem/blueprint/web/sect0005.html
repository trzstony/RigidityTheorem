If \(\operatorname {Bias}(S) \ge 2\sqrt{2} - \varepsilon \) and

\[ \Psi = \mathrm{regSwap}\bigl((V_A \otimes V_B)\psi \bigr), \]

then

\[ \Re \langle \Psi , (K \otimes \mathrm{Id})\Psi \rangle \ge 2\sqrt{2} - \delta (\varepsilon ). \]
Proof ▼

This is the bookkeeping-heavy step formalized in ‘chsh_link.lean‘. The exact \(A_0\) and \(B_0\) identities contribute no error, while the approximate \(A_1\) and \(B_1\) relations contribute the \(\sqrt{\varepsilon }\) losses absorbed into \(\delta (\varepsilon )\).
