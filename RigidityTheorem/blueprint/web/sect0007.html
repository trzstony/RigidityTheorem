1.7 Continuity of guessing probability

Lemma 16 Decision-operator form of \(p_{\mathrm{guess}}\)
#

Uses

  • Definition 5

Lean declarations

  • RigidityToPguess.pguess_decision

For a classical–quantum state \(\rho _{XE}\) with \(X\in \{ 0,1\} \),

\[ p_{\mathrm{guess}}(X\mid E)_\rho = \sup _{0\preceq \Pi \preceq I_{XE}} \operatorname {Tr}(\Pi \rho _{XE}), \]

where the supremum is over operators of the form \(\Pi =|0\rangle \! \langle 0|\otimes M_0 + |1\rangle \! \langle 1|\otimes M_1\) with \(\{ M_0,M_1\} \) a POVM on \(E\).

Proof ▼

Expanding Definition 5 gives \(\sum _x\operatorname {Tr}(M_x\rho _E^x)=\operatorname {Tr}(\Pi \rho _{XE})\) with the stated \(\Pi \). Conversely, any such \(\Pi \) corresponds to a valid POVM. Taking the supremum over POVMs is the same as taking the supremum over such \(\Pi \).

Lemma 17 Continuity of \(p_{\mathrm{guess}}\)
#

Uses

  • Lemma 16

Lean declarations

  • RigidityToPguess.pguess_continuity

For any two classical–quantum states \(\rho _{XE}\) and \(\sigma _{XE}\),

\[ p_{\mathrm{guess}}(X\mid E)_\rho \le p_{\mathrm{guess}}(X\mid E)_\sigma + \frac12\| \rho _{XE}-\sigma _{XE}\| _1. \]
Proof ▼

Set \(\Delta :=\rho _{XE}-\sigma _{XE}\). Since \(\rho _{XE}\) and \(\sigma _{XE}\) are states, \(\Delta \) is Hermitian and \(\operatorname {Tr}(\Delta )=0\).

Fix any feasible decision operator \(\Pi \). Then

\[ \operatorname {Tr}(\Pi \rho _{XE}) = \operatorname {Tr}(\Pi \sigma _{XE}) + \operatorname {Tr}(\Pi \Delta ). \]

Write the Jordan decomposition \(\Delta =\Delta _+-\Delta _-\) with \(\Delta _\pm \succeq 0\) and orthogonal supports. Because \(\operatorname {Tr}(\Delta )=0\),

\[ \operatorname {Tr}(\Delta _+)=\operatorname {Tr}(\Delta _-)=\frac12\| \Delta \| _1. \]

Since \(0\preceq \Pi \preceq I\) and \(\Delta _-\succeq 0\),

\[ \operatorname {Tr}(\Pi \Delta )=\operatorname {Tr}(\Pi \Delta _+)-\operatorname {Tr}(\Pi \Delta _-)\le \operatorname {Tr}(\Pi \Delta _+)\le \operatorname {Tr}(\Delta _+)=\frac12\| \Delta \| _1. \]

Therefore

\[ \operatorname {Tr}(\Pi \rho _{XE})\le \operatorname {Tr}(\Pi \sigma _{XE})+\frac12\| \rho _{XE}-\sigma _{XE}\| _1. \]

Now take the supremum over feasible \(\Pi \) and use Lemma 16.

Lemma 18 Guessing probability for a uniform and independent bit
#

Uses

  • Definition 5

Lean declarations

  • RigidityToPguess.pguess_uniform

Let \(\tau _E\) be any state. If

\[ \sigma _{XE}=\frac{I_X}{2}\otimes \tau _E, \]

then \(p_{\mathrm{guess}}(X\mid E)_\sigma =\tfrac 12\).

Proof ▼

For any POVM \(\{ M_0,M_1\} \), one has

\[ \sum _x \operatorname {Tr}(M_x\sigma _E^x)=\frac12\operatorname {Tr}(M_0\tau _E)+\frac12\operatorname {Tr}(M_1\tau _E)=\frac12\operatorname {Tr}((M_0+M_1)\tau _E)=\frac12. \]

Taking the supremum gives \(p_{\mathrm{guess}}=\tfrac 12\).
