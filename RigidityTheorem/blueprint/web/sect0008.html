1.8 Formalization Map

The current file-to-blueprint correspondence is:

  • ‘Quantum/Bell.lean‘: Bell basis, Bell state, and the spectrum of the canonical operator \(K\);

  • ‘Approximate_Rigidity/BasicDefs.lean‘: CHSH strategies, observables, tensor-isometry utilities, and the operator \(\mathrm{CHSH}\);

  • ‘Approximate_Rigidity/Isometries.lean‘: the constructions of \(V_A\) and \(V_B\) plus the exact \(A_0\) and \(B_0\) intertwining relations;

  • ‘Approximate_Rigidity/IsometriesApprox.lean‘: approximate anti-commutation and the approximate \(A_1\) and \(B_1\) extraction bounds;

  • ‘Approximate_Rigidity/chsh_link.lean‘: the comparison between the physical CHSH bias and the ideal Bell-space operator \(K\);

  • ‘Approximate_Rigidity/Junk.lean‘: the Bell-decomposition estimate and the abstract spectral state-extraction theorem;

  • ‘Approximate_Rigidity/RigidityTheorem.lean‘: the final state and operator extraction theorems.

This blueprint is deliberately aligned with the current Lean implementation rather than a larger paper draft. That keeps the dependency graph short and makes it easier to update as the formalization evolves.
