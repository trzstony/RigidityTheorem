Let \(\rho _{ABE}\) be a tripartite state with qubit registers \(A,B\) and arbitrary \(E\), and let \(\rho _{AB}=\operatorname {Tr}_E(\rho _{ABE})\). Assume

\[ \varepsilon \le 2\sqrt2-2, \qquad \operatorname {Tr}(\rho _{AB}\, \mathrm{CHSH})\ge 2\sqrt2-\varepsilon , \]

where \(\mathrm{CHSH}\) is the canonical two-qubit operator from Definition 3. Let \(X\) be the computational-basis measurement outcome on \(A\) as in Definition 4. Then

\[ p_{\mathrm{guess}}(X\mid E)_\rho \le \frac12 + \sqrt{\frac{\varepsilon }{2\sqrt2}} + \frac{\varepsilon }{2\sqrt2}. \]

In particular, \(p_{\mathrm{guess}}(X\mid E)_\rho \le \tfrac 12 + O(\sqrt\varepsilon )\).

Proof ▼

Set \(\Phi =|\Phi ^+\rangle \! \langle \Phi ^+|\) and define \(p:=\langle \Phi ^+|\rho _{AB}|\Phi ^+\rangle \) and \(\delta :=1-p\). By Lemma 8, \(\delta \le \varepsilon /(2\sqrt2)\). Since \(\varepsilon \le 2\sqrt2-2\), we have

\[ \delta \le \frac{\varepsilon }{2\sqrt2} \le 1-\frac{1}{\sqrt2}{\lt}1, \]

so \(p=1-\delta {\gt}0\).

Consider the projector \(\Pi :=\Phi \otimes I_E\) and define the postselected state \(\widetilde\rho _{ABE}=\Pi \rho _{ABE}\Pi /p\) as in Definition 9. Since \(\operatorname {Tr}(\Pi \rho _{ABE})=\operatorname {Tr}(\Phi \rho _{AB})=p\), Lemma 10 gives a state \(\tau _E\) such that \(\widetilde\rho _{ABE}=\Phi \otimes \tau _E\).

Let \(\rho _{XE}\) be induced from \(\rho _{ABE}\) and let \(\sigma _{XE}\) be induced from \(\widetilde\rho _{ABE}\) by measuring \(A\) in the computational basis and discarding \(B\). From \(\widetilde\rho _{ABE}=\Phi \otimes \tau _E\), measuring \(A\) yields a uniform bit independent of \(E\), hence \(\sigma _{XE}=\tfrac {I_X}{2}\otimes \tau _E\) and \(p_{\mathrm{guess}}(X\mid E)_\sigma =\tfrac 12\) by Lemma 18.

By Lemma 17,

\[ p_{\mathrm{guess}}(X\mid E)_\rho \le \frac12 + \frac12\| \rho _{XE}-\sigma _{XE}\| _1. \]

By Lemma 15 and Lemma 11,

\[ \| \rho _{XE}-\sigma _{XE}\| _1 \le \| \rho _{ABE}-\widetilde\rho _{ABE}\| _1 \le 2\sqrt{1-p}+2(1-p)=2\sqrt\delta +2\delta . \]

Combining gives

\[ p_{\mathrm{guess}}(X\mid E)_\rho \le \frac12 + \sqrt\delta +\delta \le \frac12 + \sqrt{\frac{\varepsilon }{2\sqrt2}} + \frac{\varepsilon }{2\sqrt2}. \]
